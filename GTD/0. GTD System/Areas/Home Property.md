# Area: Home & Property Management

## Purpose
Maintain comfortable, functional, and well-organized living spaces. Ensure property is well-maintained, secure, and meets current and future needs.

## Standards
- Regular maintenance schedules followed
- Home is clean, organized, and comfortable
- Safety and security measures in place
- Utilities and systems functioning properly
- Space optimized for productivity and relaxation
