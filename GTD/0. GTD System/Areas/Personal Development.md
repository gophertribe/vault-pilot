# Area: Personal Development & Health

## Purpose
Maintain and improve physical health, mental well-being, and personal skills. This includes fitness, mindfulness, learning new skills, and maintaining work-life balance.

## Standards
- Regular exercise routine maintained
- Mindfulness/meditation practice consistent
- Continuous learning in areas of interest
- Stress levels manageable
- Good sleep and nutrition habits
